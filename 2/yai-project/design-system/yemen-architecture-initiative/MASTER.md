## 4. Key effects

- Focus rings: `outline: 3px solid #0369A1; outline-offset: 2px` (WCAG AAA)
- Transitions: `150–300ms` ease-out, transform/opacity only
- Touch targets: minimum `44×44px`
- Hover cards: `translateY(-2px)` + subtle shadow (no layout shift)
- Skeleton loaders: pulse animation for async content

---

## 5. Anti-patterns to avoid

- No AI purple/pink gradients
- No ornate decorative borders (this is architecture, not decoration)
- No low contrast text (4.5:1 minimum, 7:1 target)
- No motion effects without reduced-motion fallback
- No Arabic text in LTR containers (always wrap in `dir="rtl"`)
- No generic stock photography — use only real Yemeni architectural photography
